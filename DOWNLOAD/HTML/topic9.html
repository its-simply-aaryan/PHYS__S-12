Working my way around the LAN server proved to be more challenging than I had expected it to be. I kept getting question marks on my serial monitor. FIREBASE

Compared to the LAN server, working my way around firebase was comparatively more comfortable. When I followed the tutorial and set up an LED to pin 5, I figured that my LED was not in synch with my Firebase. After a lot of thinking, I realized that I had made two errors:

  1. I did not change to the Real Time Database
  2. I did not change the Read and Write Statements in the Database to true from false


After correcting these errors, my LED worked perfectly well with my Firebase. Here is a link to the Firebase tutorial page.
After this, I tried to get my Firebase to work with a website, which is obviously a more comfortable UI. However, somehow my Firebase did not work through my website. I' still trying to figure out what went wrong.
