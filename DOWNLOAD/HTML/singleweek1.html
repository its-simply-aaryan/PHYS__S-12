PROJECT 'Cleaning Bot'

This project is for the creation of a vaccum cleaning bot that is controlled through an app on your phone. A sensor would help it avoid collisions and estimate jumps such as in case of stairs. Responses would be voice-controlled. The bot would ideally be for two purposes - dry vaccum and mopping the floor.

The same bot could be used as a transportation instrument. The map or floor plan of the location can be fed into the system. With a command, the bot would just got to that location. It makes it easier, let's say to send food from the kitchen to the dining room.
