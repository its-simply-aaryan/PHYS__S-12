2D Design and Cutting

Introduction to laser cutting & engraving, Vinyl cutter, Inkscape & Gimp

INTRODUCTION TO LASER CUTTING & ENGRAVING

What I've learnt from this class is that a laser cutter is basically an instrument used to cut materials with the help of laser and optical equippment by the passage of a high power volatge. I have also learnt that the speed of the laser cutter can be altered by the amount of power supplied, but we must draw a line somewhere with reference to the material used because excess power may start a fire. Here is a list of materials that can be used for a laser cutter. Even though this istrument can be used to cut absoulutely anything, we must never cut PVC plastic and ABS Plastic. I've also learnt about the kerf that must be calculated while making the vector lines to cover up for the additional material used up during laser cutting. This is done in order to mould the perfect fit. We did not discuss much about the technicalities of the engraving process but the method is not much different from that of cutting. The only difference is the machine does not cut deep through the entire material but just engraves a picture on top.

INTRODUCTION TO VINYL CUTTERS, INKSCAPE & GIMP

For this lecture, we saw more examples then diving into the technicalities. We saw live examples of vinyl cut products and also saw examples of Vinyl stickers. What I have learnt is that a vinyl cutter is a computer controlled device that is used to cut shapes and ppatterns over a thin self-adhesive plastic. Inkscape & Gimp are softwares, the former being an open source vector graphics software and the latter being an open source image processing program. Both of these are used to develop files that are input into the vinyl cutter.

Readings & Documentation

This week's readings mainly circled around electricity. 3 questions were adressed:
What is electricity?
Most of this part of the reading was a revision for what I learnt in the first semester of my senior year. Yet, it was useful as I plan to work with circuits for my final project.
What is a circuit?
I left reading one with aq question in my mind and 'voila', reading 2 adresses the very same question. Circuits 101 is a tutorial that explains voltage in great detail. Along with explanation of various components of a circuit, it also gave precautions and tips here and there. I think this reading will be very useful for me while making my final project with direct applications of what I've read.
Electric Power!
With the help of this tutorial, I learnt how to calculate power using volatge, current and resistance along with maximum power ratings.
